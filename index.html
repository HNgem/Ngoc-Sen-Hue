In the ancient capital of Hue, with its poetic rivers and vibrant lotus gardens, a story unfolds about Ngoc Sen Hue. This journey began with pure lotus seeds, blended with modern freeze-drying technology to create products of both nutritional value and Vietnamese artistry. Established on October 10, 2017. Ngoc Sen Hue represents a union of wisdom and love for the homeland, carrying a heartfelt mission: to bring natural lotus treasures to everyone, fostering health and happiness for families everywhere.

Ngoc Sen Hue is the origin of lotus seeds that embody not only nutritional value but also the essence of a distinctive land and culture. Constantly innovating and developing, the company produces high-quality products that meet the strictest standards, showcasing the beauty of Hue's lotus seeds to the world and elevating the global value of Vietnamese lotus seeds.

Ngoc Sen Hue – A natural essence, where lotus seeds transcend time, bringing purity, elegance, and completeness to life.
